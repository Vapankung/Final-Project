Historical Thai Dance show (Rabam boranakadee) have invented from Mr.Thanit Yoopho idea, former Director-General of the Fine Arts Department. He had inspired by visiting historical site from Thailand and nearby country and had seen painting, sculpture and etc. In 1967,Mr.Thanit have invented new instruments and new Thai Dance pose. He asked several people to help him such as, Mr. Montri Tramote, an expert in Thai music and national artists compose a melody according to his concept. Mrs. Lamul Yamakup, Mrs. Chaley Sukhavanich and Lady Phaew Sanitwongsenee, invented Thai Dance pose for this show. Historical Thai Dance show consists of 5 set:

  1. Dwarawadee Dance(6th-11th century)
  2. Srivijaya Dance(8th-13th century)
  3. Lop buri Dance(11th-14th century)
  4. Srivijaya Dance(12th-20th century)
  5. Srivijaya Dance(14th-15th century)
